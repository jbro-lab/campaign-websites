## Image Recommendations — Template 6: Grassroots

### Hero Background Image
- **Size:** 1920 × 1080px (landscape, full-width)
- **Usage:** Full-width background behind hero text, with dark gradient overlay
- **Style:** Community scene — rally, town hall, neighborhood event, or local landmark
- **Notes:** Image will be darkened with an overlay; choose a high-contrast image that works well dimmed

### About Photo
- **Size:** 500 × 600px (portrait)
- **Usage:** Left column of the About section
- **Style:** Warm, approachable — candidate at a community event, talking to neighbors, or a professional headshot

### General Tips
- Use 2x dimensions for retina displays (e.g., 3840 × 2160 for hero)
- Keep files under 500KB (compress with TinyPNG or similar)
- Match filenames in `content.json` exactly (capitalization matters)
- JPG recommended for photos; PNG for images with transparency
